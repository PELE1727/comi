NEW MINERALS DISCOVERED

COMIKAGI–Kababara mine concession has an Amblygonite deposit, a high-grade lithium-bearing fluorophosphate mineral containing roughly 8–10% lithium oxide, often surpassing spodumene (6%) and lepidolite (3%) in concentration. Found in granite pegmatites, it is a white-to-pale-colored triclinic mineral sometimes used as a minor but high-value source of lithium.
