OUR WORK

MINING METHOD

Underground mining methods in COMIKAGI are based on extracting mineral deposits beneath the surface in pegmatite ores hosted in schist and metasedimentary rocks by creating tunnels, shafts, and stopes, primarily using methods like room-and-pillar. This technique is selected based on orebody shape, depth, and strength to optimize safety, recovery, and cost-efficiency.

MINERAL FIELD PROCESSING

Mineral field processing, often referred to as mineral processing, mineral dressing, or beneficiation in the initial stage of extractive, separates valuable minerals from their ore (waste rock) directly at the mine site through sluice. It serves to upgrade the economic value of the mined material, producing a high-grade concentrate and a waste stream known as tailings.

SECURITY

Security means safety, as well as the measures taken to be safe or protected of mined minerals from the field to the office up to the market.
